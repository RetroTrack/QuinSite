What the mod contains

A Simple summary -> it has more!

CUstom colors

Show your skill with the green color, telling everybody you haven't died once, or tell everybody you died with the red color, and that you've been revived with yellow!

Orb of Revival

Revive your dead friends and maybe even your dad, using the Orb Of Revival!

‍

Revival Menu

Choose who you'd like to revive in this menu, which of course has different themes and can be modified by texture packs!
